About Tupie

Tupie comes from the Swahili words Tupa (throw) and Mie (give it to me). Instead of throwing away useful items, you can give them a new life by sharing with someone in need.

Our Mission

Tupie helps reduce waste and promotes community support by connecting people who have unwanted items with those who truly need them. Our platform empowers sustainable living and strengthens local communities.
